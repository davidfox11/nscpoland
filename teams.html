THE OFFICIALS OF TROJMIASTO 2019

Below you will find all of the wonderful people who are helping to shape this session in very different ways. The organising team is taking care of every logisitical aspect of the session, and the team has been working hard to make the session a reality for almost a year. The chairs team will shape the academic quality of the session, and help the delegates to express their ideas and encourahe lively debates in committee work. The media team are responsible for the creative content of the session, and they will help you to keep this session in your memory long after the closing ceremony. The Jury team will be there to analyse your progress throughout the session.

Behind each team is a great leadership, and this can be seen in the dedication of our team leaders, who you can see below along with the rest of the team members.
